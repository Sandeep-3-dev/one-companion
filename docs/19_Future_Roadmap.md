---

## Vision Context

This document presents the long-term strategic vision for the future evolution of ONE COMPANION.

Rather than representing a fixed technology solution, ONE COMPANION is designed as an evolving Enterprise AI platform capable of continuously expanding its intelligence, capabilities, and business impact while preserving the stability, security, and reliability of SBI's existing banking ecosystem.

The Future Roadmap demonstrates how Enterprise AI becomes a long-term strategic capability that continuously enhances customer experience, operational excellence, business intelligence, and sustainable digital transformation.

---

# 1. Future Vision

The long-term vision of ONE COMPANION is to become SBI's intelligent financial companion throughout every stage of every customer's financial journey.

Future evolution focuses on:

- Continuous Enterprise AI innovation.
- Intelligent customer experiences.
- Autonomous financial assistance.
- Enterprise-wide intelligence.
- Sustainable business transformation.
- Responsible AI adoption.

This vision positions ONE COMPANION as an Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer that continuously strengthens SBI's banking ecosystem without replacing its existing enterprise systems.

# 2. Enterprise Evolution Independence

ONE COMPANION is designed to continuously evolve without requiring fundamental changes to SBI's existing Core Banking Systems.

This evolution model enables:

- Independent capability expansion.
- Independent Enterprise AI innovation.
- Independent customer experience improvements.
- Independent business capability evolution.
- Independent technology modernization.

This approach ensures ONE COMPANION remains future-ready while protecting SBI's long-term technology investments.
