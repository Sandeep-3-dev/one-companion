# Customer Lifecycle

---

## Architecture Context

This document explains how ONE COMPANION manages the complete banking customer lifecycle through a unified Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer. Instead of treating customer acquisition, onboarding, engagement, and retention as isolated business processes, the platform orchestrates them as one continuous intelligent lifecycle that evolves alongside every customer's financial journey.

---

# 1. Customer Lifecycle Overview

Modern banking systems often manage customer acquisition, onboarding, servicing, and engagement as independent operational workflows. This fragmented approach creates disconnected customer experiences, inconsistent personalization, and limited long-term relationship building.

ONE COMPANION redefines this approach by managing every customer interaction through a unified lifecycle powered by a centralized Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer.

Rather than optimizing individual customer touchpoints, the platform continuously learns from every interaction, enabling increasingly personalized, proactive, and intelligent banking experiences throughout the customer's relationship with SBI.

# 2. Enterprise Customer Lifecycle Stages

The lifecycle is organized into six continuous enterprise stages.

| Lifecycle Stage | Primary Objective |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Acquire** | Identify and engage high-potential customers through AI-powered acquisition. |
| **Onboard** | Complete secure customer onboarding using existing SBI verification and compliance systems. |
| **Understand** | Build and continuously enrich a unified Customer 360° profile. |
| **Engage** | Deliver proactive financial guidance, recommendations, and personalized experiences. |
| **Retain** | Strengthen long-term customer relationships through intelligent engagement and continuous value creation. |
| **Learn** | Continuously improve enterprise intelligence through behavioral learning and AI optimization. |

Each stage contributes new intelligence back into the Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer, enabling progressively better customer understanding and decision-making.

---

# 3. Enterprise Customer Lifecycle Flow

### Acquire

The lifecycle begins before a customer becomes an SBI account holder.

AI-powered customer acquisition identifies high-intent prospects through intelligent campaigns, conversational engagement, behavioral analysis, and lead qualification.

---

### Onboard

Qualified prospects are guided through secure onboarding integrated with SBI's existing identity verification, authentication, and compliance processes.

The Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer assists throughout onboarding while ensuring enterprise governance and regulatory compliance.

---

### Understand

Following successful onboarding, customer information from enterprise banking systems is consolidated into a unified Customer 360° profile.

This continuously evolving profile establishes the intelligence foundation for every future AI-driven decision.

---

### Engage

Using Customer 360° intelligence, the platform proactively delivers personalized recommendations, financial reports, simulations, multilingual assistance, and contextual financial guidance tailored to each customer's evolving financial journey.

---

### Retain

Long-term relationships are strengthened through personalized engagement strategies, financial wellness initiatives, AI-driven referral optimization, Family Banking Mode, and continuous customer value creation.

---

### Learn

Every interaction continuously improves enterprise intelligence.

Behavioral insights, customer feedback, and engagement outcomes are analyzed to refine Customer 360°, improve AI reasoning, optimize future recommendations, and strengthen the overall banking ecosystem.

---

# 4. Lifecycle State Transitions

The customer lifecycle progresses through clearly defined enterprise state transitions.

| Current Stage | Transition Trigger | Next Stage |
|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Prospect | Successful AI Qualification | Onboard |
| Onboard | Account Successfully Created | Understand |
| Understand | Customer 360° Established | Engage |
| Engage | Continuous Personalized Experiences | Retain |
| Retain | Customer Interactions & Behavioral Learning | Learn |
| Learn | Updated Enterprise Intelligence | Continuous Lifecycle Improvement |

This structured progression ensures every customer advances through a controlled, intelligence-driven lifecycle while continuously enriching enterprise knowledge.

---

# 5. Role of the Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer

The Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer remains active throughout every lifecycle stage.

Rather than participating only during customer engagement, it continuously analyzes customer behavior, orchestrates enterprise workflows, updates Customer 360° intelligence, validates AI decisions through enterprise governance, and coordinates personalized experiences across the complete banking lifecycle.

This shared intelligence model ensures every lifecycle phase benefits from accumulated customer knowledge instead of operating independently, creating a continuously improving banking experience.

---

# 6. Lifecycle Intelligence Model

Unlike traditional banking workflows that treat customer interactions as isolated events, ONE COMPANION manages the lifecycle as a continuous intelligence cycle.

        ↓

This closed-loop intelligence model enables the platform to become increasingly accurate, personalized, and valuable throughout every customer's financial journey.

# 8. Lifecycle Design Principles

The customer lifecycle is guided by the following enterprise principles:

- **Customer-Centric Intelligence** – Every lifecycle stage is personalized around the individual customer.
- **Continuous Learning** – Customer understanding continuously evolves throughout the banking relationship.
- **Proactive Engagement** – Financial assistance is delivered before customers actively request support.
- **Seamless Enterprise Integration** – Every lifecycle stage operates within SBI's existing enterprise ecosystem.
- **Responsible AI Governance** – Every AI-driven interaction complies with enterprise security, governance, compliance, and business policies.

Together, these principles establish a scalable, intelligent, and future-ready customer lifecycle capable of supporting enterprise banking at scale.
