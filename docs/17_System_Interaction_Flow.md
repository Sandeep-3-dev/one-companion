# Enterprise System Interaction Architecture

---

## Architecture Context

This document defines how enterprise capabilities within ONE COMPANION interact to deliver intelligent, secure, governed, and scalable banking experiences.

Rather than operating as isolated systems, Customer360°, Enterprise AI Memory Framework, Enterprise AI Intelligence, the Enterprise Multi-Agent Architecture, Business Capability Services, and Core Banking Systems collaborate through governed enterprise interactions.

The Enterprise System Interaction Architecture provides a unified operational model that enables every customer request to follow a consistent, explainable, secure, and enterprise-governed interaction lifecycle while preserving the stability of SBI's existing banking ecosystem.

---

# 1. Enterprise Interaction Strategy

ONE COMPANION follows a **Capability-Oriented Enterprise Interaction Strategy**.

Every customer interaction is processed through coordinated enterprise capabilities rather than isolated applications or independent AI services.

The strategy focuses on:

- Unified enterprise interactions
- Intelligent capability orchestration
- Governed decision execution
- Explainable customer experiences
- Enterprise operational consistency
- Continuous learning and improvement

This strategy enables every enterprise capability to contribute to a seamless, intelligent, and governed banking experience.

---

# 2. Enterprise Interaction Independence

Customer interaction workflows operate independently from Core Banking operations while remaining fully governed through Business Capability Services.

This interaction model enables:

- Independent evolution of customer experiences.
- Independent Enterprise AI improvements.
- Independent workflow optimization.
- Independent interaction scalability.
- Independent operational monitoring.

Business Capability Services remain the controlled execution layer responsible for all governed banking operations.

This separation enables SBI to continuously improve customer interactions without disrupting existing Core Banking Systems.

---

# 3. Enterprise Interaction Principles

The Enterprise System Interaction Architecture follows seven core principles.

### Unified Customer Journey

Every customer interaction follows one governed enterprise workflow regardless of the access channel.

---

### Capability Collaboration

Enterprise capabilities collaborate according to clearly defined responsibilities.

---

### Explainable Processing

Every enterprise decision remains transparent, traceable, and auditable.

---

### Governed Execution

Banking operations execute only after enterprise governance and policy validation.

---

### Separation of Responsibilities

Every enterprise capability performs only its designated responsibility.

---

### Operational Consistency

Interactions remain consistent across all enterprise customer channels.

---

### Continuous Learning

Every completed interaction strengthens future enterprise intelligence and customer experiences.

# 5. Enterprise Interaction Participants

| Enterprise Participant | Primary Responsibility |
|-------------------------|------------------------|
| **Customer** | Initiate enterprise interactions |
| **Customer Channels** | Provide secure customer access |
| **Enterprise API Gateway** | Secure enterprise entry point |
| **Enterprise Integration Layer** | Connect enterprise capabilities |
| **Communication Agent** | Understand customer intent and interactions |
| **Enterprise Orchestration Agent** | Coordinate enterprise workflows |
| **Customer360° Enterprise Intelligence** | Provide long-term customer intelligence |
| **Enterprise AI Memory Framework** | Maintain contextual intelligence throughout interactions |
| **Enterprise AI Intelligence Layer** | Generate enterprise insights and recommendations |
| **Enterprise AI Decision Framework** | Evaluate enterprise decisions |
| **Enterprise Compliance Agent** | Validate governance, regulatory policies, and compliance |
| **Business Capability Services** | Execute approved banking capabilities |
| **Core Banking Systems** | Execute financial transactions and maintain banking records |

Each participant performs a clearly defined enterprise responsibility while collaborating through governed enterprise interactions.

---

# 6. Enterprise Interaction Boundaries

Enterprise interactions follow clearly defined operational boundaries.

| Enterprise Capability | Interaction Responsibility | Interaction Restriction |
|------------------------|----------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Customer Channels** | Initiate customer interactions | Cannot execute banking operations |
| **Enterprise API Gateway** | Secure enterprise entry point | Cannot make business decisions |
| **Enterprise Integration Layer** | Route enterprise interactions | Cannot modify business rules |
| **Enterprise AI Platform** | Generate enterprise intelligence | Cannot execute banking transactions |
| **Business Capability Services** | Execute approved business capabilities | Cannot bypass enterprise governance |
| **Core Banking Systems** | Execute financial transactions | Cannot modify Enterprise AI decisions |

These boundaries ensure every enterprise interaction remains secure, governed, explainable, and operationally consistent.
